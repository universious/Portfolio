About Me

Hey, I’m Shaan! I started Designing as a hobby ,but over time,I realized how much I enjoy it. There’s something exciting about turning an idea into a visual piece - seeing it come to life on the screen and knowing I made this.

I love the creative side of design, especially how certain visuals can be appealing to the eye. My style isn’t tied to one look - It really depends on what i’m creating. Some projects turn out dark and moody,while others are playful and vibrant. I also enjoy experimenting and trying out new techniques when i can.

Right now, I’m focusing on learning more and improving my skills,Most of my work comes from personal projects and random ideas that pop into my head, but i’m always open to new challenges , looking forward to work using everything i’ve taught myself and learn more stuff
